Z1 Winterfest 2025

Instructions

  • Click on "Register" to begin.
  • You can follow the guided process, to fill and submit participation details of all family members in your flat at once.
  • When you are done, go to Checkout tab, review the details and proceed with submission.
  • Last date to submit the form is Monday, 3rd November 2025.

Fees

  • Participation fee is Rs.700/- per flat. Irrespective of the number of submissions from your flat, you need to pay this fee only once.
  • For Master Chef (18+), there is a refundable fee of Rs.1500/- per participant. This is to ensure commitment to the competition as it requires several provisions to be arranged.
  • If you wish to host a Food stall, the fee is Rs. 800/- per day per stall (on first come first serve basis).

Acknowledgement

  General Rules
  • Participants must be residents of Z1.
  • Participants can only play in their own age category as specified for that event.
  • For age validation (if required), organizers may request for age proof of participants.
  • Participants must arrive on time as per schedule.
  • If an event doesn't have a minimum of 4 individual/ team participation, it will be canceled.
  • Participants from the senior most age category can choose to drop down to the previous age category.
  • Each contest will have a Winner and a Runners-up. The decision of Referee/ Umpire/ Linesman/ Judge will be final.
  Racket games
  • Racket games (Badminton, Lawn Tennis and Table Tennis) will be conducted as a separate event in the month of January 2026.
